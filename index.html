Our Mission

Clean waters through intelligent robotics

FluidMotion Robotics LLC is pioneering autonomous water cleaning solutions for marinas, lakes, and pools across the United States. Our Trash Feaster Vessel (TFV) platform combines AI navigation with automated waste collection to keep water environments clean without human intervention.

Founded by experienced engineers from leading autonomous vehicle companies, we're developing commercial-ready vessels that work 24/7 to remove floating debris, plastic waste, and organic materials. Our vessels safely operate around boats, swimmers, and wildlife while efficiently cleaning water surfaces.

Starting with US marinas, lakes, and pool facilities in 2027, we're expanding to ocean cleaning applications as our technology scales. Our business model includes vessel sales, leasing programs, and comprehensive maintenance services for water facility operators and environmental organizations.

Our Technology

Cutting-edge autonomous systems for marine environments

DPilot Navigation System

Patent-pending autonomous navigation system that enables vessels to operate independently in marinas, lakes, and pools. Features GPS positioning, obstacle detection, and wireless communication for reliable unmanned operation and remote monitoring capabilities.

Trash Feaster Vessels (TFV)

Autonomous catamaran-hull vessels with dual 360° rotating jet thrusters, integrated waste collection systems, and Return-to-Base (R2B) functionality. Unsinkable design with anti-capsize features, solar charging, and docking mechanisms for automated operation.

Smart Water Cleaning

AI-powered waste detection and collection system that identifies floating debris, plastic waste, and organic materials. Smart navigation safely avoids boats, swimmers, and wildlife while efficiently cleaning water surfaces in marinas, lakes, and pools.

Reliable Marine Operation

Purpose-built for water environments with weatherproof design, solar charging, and automatic docking for waste disposal. Operates continuously with minimal maintenance, designed specifically for US marina and lake conditions.
